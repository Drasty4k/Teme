Merci 🌕 Bonsoir 👌

👻 Ciao Bye Bye

2020 vingt-vingt

A+ Peace Out

Ingredients*

  • 1.5 Oz --------- Of Bitter Liqueur, Campari
  • 4 Oz --------- Fresh Orange Juice
  • 1 Orange Slice For Topping

*

Please Note That These Ingredients produce A Magnificent Linear-gradient(#edb14c, #b63b30)

Long Story Short.

An old English proverb says 'early to bed and early to rise make a man healthy, wealthy and wise', but what do we do once we're awake... During the Christmas season, we only have one word for you: Garibaldi. This cocktail only has two ingredients and is perfect at any time be it brunch or cocktail hour.

Steps

  • 01 Squeeze the juice of two oranges and pour the juice intro a blender.
  • 02 Mix until an aerial texture is obtained.
  • 03 Pour the campari into a glass filled with ice.
  • 04 Top with an orange quarter
